## Recommended image themes

### 1. Skyline shots (hero background)

**What to look for:**
- Downtown skyline from Mount Washington or the Duquesne Incline
- Golden hour/sunset lighting (warm, inviting)
- Point State Park with the fountain visible
- Three rivers confluence (Allegheny, Monongahela, Ohio)

**Search terms:**
- "Pittsburgh skyline sunset"
- "Pittsburgh downtown aerial"
- "Pittsburgh Mount Washington view"
- "Point State Park fountain"

**Best for:** Hero section background, email headers, social media banners

---

### 2. Bridges (symbolizing collaboration)

**Why bridges work:** Pittsburgh is the "City of Bridges" (446 bridges!)—a perfect metaphor for collaborative journalism connecting communities.

**Iconic bridges to feature:**
- **Roberto Clemente Bridge** (yellow, pedestrian-friendly)
- **Rachel Carson Bridge** (distinctive yellow arches)
- **Fort Duquesne Bridge** (dramatic angles)
- **Smithfield Street Bridge** (historic, ornate)
- **Andy Warhol Bridge** (named for the Pittsburgh native)

**Search terms:**
- "Pittsburgh bridges"
- "Roberto Clemente Bridge"
- "Pittsburgh yellow bridges"
- "Allegheny River bridges"

**Best for:** Section dividers, "10 years of connection" visuals, partnership imagery

---

### 3. Neighborhoods and streets

**For local flavor:**
- **Strip District** - Produce markets, urban energy
- **Lawrenceville** - Arts, murals, coffee shops
- **Oakland** - University of Pittsburgh, Carnegie Museums
- **South Side** - Historic architecture, Carson Street
- **Downtown** - PPG Place, Market Square

**Search terms:**
- "Pittsburgh Strip District"
- "Pittsburgh street scene"
- "Pittsburgh murals"
- "Market Square Pittsburgh"

**Best for:** "Why Pittsburgh" section, venue area imagery, local context

---

### 4. Innovation and technology

**Pittsburgh's reinvention story:**
- Carnegie Mellon University robotics
- Tech startups in converted warehouses
- The Mattress Factory (art museum)
- Phipps Conservatory (sustainability)

**Search terms:**
- "Pittsburgh innovation"
- "Carnegie Mellon campus"
- "Pittsburgh tech"

**Best for:** "Future of journalism" themes, innovation narratives

---

### 5. Rivers and water

**The three rivers define Pittsburgh:**
- Kayaking on the Allegheny
- Riverfront trails
- Point State Park fountain
- River cruise views

**Search terms:**
- "Pittsburgh rivers"
- "Three rivers Pittsburgh"
- "Point State Park"
- "Allegheny River"

**Best for:** Relaxed, natural imagery; evening event promotions

## Specific image recommendations

### Hero section background

**Option 1: Classic skyline**
- Search: "Pittsburgh skyline golden hour"
- Look for: Warm sunset tones, downtown visible, bridges in frame
- Mood: Welcoming, celebratory

**Option 2: Bridge perspective**
- Search: "Roberto Clemente Bridge Pittsburgh"
- Look for: Looking across bridge toward downtown
- Mood: Forward motion, crossing into something new

**Option 3: Aerial view**
- Search: "Pittsburgh aerial downtown"
- Look for: Birds-eye view of the Point, rivers converging
- Mood: Big picture, comprehensive view

### Cards and thumbnails

**For "What to expect" cards:**
- Conference/networking imagery (generic is okay)
- Or Pittsburgh-specific: coffee shop, outdoor seating, museum

**For timeline section:**
- Each year could use a subtle city reference
- Or keep abstract and use icons instead

## Image specifications

### For web use

| Location | Recommended size | Format |
|----------|------------------|--------|
| Hero background | 1920×1080px minimum | JPG (compressed) |
| Card images | 800×600px | JPG or WebP |
| Thumbnails | 400×300px | JPG or WebP |
| Social share | 1200×630px | PNG |

### Optimization tips

1. **Compress images** before uploading (use TinyPNG or Squoosh)
2. **Use WebP format** when possible (30% smaller than JPG)
3. **Add alt text** for accessibility
4. **Consider lazy loading** for below-fold images
