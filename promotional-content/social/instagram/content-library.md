## Instagram Platform Strategy

### Stories Strategy
- Use daily during campaign for:
  - Behind-the-scenes content
  - Polls and questions for engagement
  - Countdown stickers
  - Registration reminders
  - User-generated content reposts
  - Quick updates and announcements

### Reels Strategy
- Create 3-5 Reels throughout campaign:
  1. "What is CJS2026?" explainer (January)
  2. Venue tour of Chapel Hill (March)
  3. Speaker highlight montage (April)
  4. "Pack with me for CJS2026" (May)
  5. Event recap (June)

### Hashtag Strategy
**Primary (use on every post):**
- #CJS2026
- #CollaborativeJournalism
- #Journalism

**Secondary (rotate based on content):**
- #MediaCollaboration
- #JournalismCommunity
- #ChapelHill
- #NorthCarolina
- #JournalismSummit
- #MediaInnovation
- #10thAnniversary

**Engagement Hashtags (occasionally):**
- #JournalismLife
- #NewsroomLife
- #IndieJournalism
- #LocalJournalism

**Limit:** 15-20 hashtags per post max (Instagram allows 30 but 15-20 performs better)

### Posting Frequency
- **January-February:** 2-3 posts per week
- **March (registration open):** 3-4 posts per week
- **April-May:** 2-3 posts per week
- **June (event week):** Multiple posts daily + Stories

### Best Times to Post
- **Weekdays:** 8-10am EST or 5-7pm EST
- **Avoid:** Late night, early morning
- **Best days:** Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday

### Engagement Tactics
- Respond to all comments within 24 hours
- Like and comment on tagged posts
- Repost user-generated content to Stories
- Use location tags for Chapel Hill content
- Tag speakers and partners when relevant
- Save registration/important posts to Highlights

### Link in Bio Strategy
Update link based on campaign phase:
- January: General info page
- March: Registration page
- April: Schedule/speakers page
- May: Attendee resources
- June: Live updates/schedule

### Instagram Highlights Categories
Create these permanent Story Highlights:
1. **About CJS** - What is the summit, history
2. **2026 Info** - Event details, venue, dates
3. **Speakers** - Speaker announcements and spotlights
4. **Register** - Registration info and deadlines
5. **Travel** - Chapel Hill guide, venue info
6. **Past Events** - Throwback content from previous summits
